Professionals of Streaming

Open Call

To participate in our open call, send your material to demos@streamingcasting.com with the following information:

🎬 Required Audiovisual Material

  • Video presentation (1–3 min): who you are, what you do, experience, and goals. (See “Presentation Guide”)
  • Demo video (5–10 min): practical showcase of your specialty.
  • Extended performance video: longer video (or several short ones) showing your style and creative proposal.

📋 Presentation Guide

  1. Personal information: full name, city/country, age, contact.
  2. Background and training: studies, relevant workshops, practical experience.
  3. Specialty: e.g., streamer, cameraman, video editor, sound engineer, graphic designer, scenographer, etc.
  4. Portfolio link: YouTube, Vimeo, Instagram, TikTok, or personal website.

⚖️ Authorization Required

Before we can publish or share your material, you must sign the authorization form.
