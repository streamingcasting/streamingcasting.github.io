If you have a project and are looking for collaborators, or if you want to collaborate on the projects of other professionals as equals, this space is for you.

📋 Share Your Proposal

Develop and share your proposal with us, and we will help you find the best collaborators for your project.

You can send your proposal in text, audio, or video format. Please include:

  • Your idea in detail
  • The part you will handle yourself
  • The parts you want to delegate
  • The specialists you need
  • The percentage of profits you want to share with collaborators
  • Project start date
  • Project completion date
  • Any other relevant details

💡 Important Note

The difference between a streaming professional and an independent streaming professional is in how they are compensated:

  • Professional: charges for their services directly, with guaranteed payment.
  • Independent: invests their expertise or capital in the project as a bet. If the project succeeds, they earn a percentage of the profits. If not, they don’t get paid.
